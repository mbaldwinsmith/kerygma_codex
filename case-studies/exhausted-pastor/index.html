    Case Study: The Exhausted Pastor

    A pastoral reflection on spiritual healing and coherence repair.

    Case Study: The Exhausted Pastor

    Situation

    A pastor is depleted by constant care, conflict, and expectations. They feel responsible for everyone and ashamed to rest.

    Distortions Pressing In

    • Ministry is equated with self-erasure.
    • Success is measured by visibility and growth.
    • Vulnerability feels dangerous.

    Gentle Path (Practices)

    • Grace and Justification: identity before role.
    • Peace: restore safety and reduce pressure.
    • Sabbath and Waiting: paced recovery without guilt.
    • Lament: honest grief before God.
    • Spiritual Direction: safe, non-coercive accompaniment.

    Safeguards

    • Clear boundaries and time off are protected.
    • External accountability and care are welcomed.
    • The community shares the load; no heroics.
    • Professional support is normalized.

    Signs of Repair

    • Rest is possible without shame.
    • Leadership becomes gentler and less reactive.
    • The pastor can receive care as well as give it.

    Fails the Cross If…

    • The pastor is pressured to perform or hide weakness.
    • Growth is prioritized over safety.
    • Rest is treated as disloyalty.
