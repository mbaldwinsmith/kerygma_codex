# Living Glossary

Alignment — tuning toward truth, love, and God.  
Attractor — a repeated pattern of inner life.  
Coherence — healed, stable, cruciform love under pressure.  
Entropy — moral–relational fragmentation.  
Field — shared relational influence (not “cosmic stuff”).  
Radiance — overflow of healed coherence into others.  
Repair Ecology — a habitat where healing becomes natural.
